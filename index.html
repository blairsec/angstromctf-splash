FAQ:

What is a CTF?

A CTF, or Capture the Flag, is an online cybersecurity competition where players work in teams to solve as many challenges as possible. Challenges are services or files that you must investigate and exploit in order to obtain a string called the “flag”, which is submitted for points.

Who are you?

책ngstromCTF is run by a team of students from Montgomery Blair High School. Now in our 4th year, we are one of the largest high school-oriented CTFs, with over 1,500 competing teams in 2018.

What if I've never done anything like this before?

Then 책ngstromCTF is a great place to start! Our challenges are meant to guide students and help them develop their skills, even if they start off as a complete beginner.

I'm not a high schooler. Can I still compete?

Yup! You'll simply need to mark yourself as ineligible for prizes when signing up. Ineligible players will still be able to view challenges, submit flags, and see how they rank compared to other teams.
